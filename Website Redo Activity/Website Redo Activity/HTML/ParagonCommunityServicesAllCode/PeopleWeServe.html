People We Serve

Paragon Community Services offers programs for individuals with a wide array of disabilities and special needs.

Paragon serves youth and young adults in our Paragon Activity Club, a program which focuses on personal growth through fun activities, events, outings, and social interaction.

Paragon serves adults with:

  • Developmental Disabilities
  • Brain Injuries
  • Physical Disabilities
  • Mental Illness
  • Alzheimer’s
  • Dementia
  • Other Significant Medical and Behavioral Support Needs

Our staff of highly-trained, caring professionals has experience with a wide variety of health and personal needs services, including but not limited to:

  • Ambulation Support (i.e. wheelchairs, walkers, canes)
  • Incontinence Care (i.e. depends, toileting schedules)
  • Foley Catheterization Care
  • Condom Catheterization Care
  • Ostomy Care
  • Stoma Care
  • Oxygen Care
  • Range of Motion and Physical Therapy Follow Through
  • Feeding Assistance
  • Diabetes Care
  • Special Feeding Assistance (i.e., g-tube, one on one feeding, choking prevention, modified diets)
  • Seizure Support (Vagus Nerve Stimulators, PRN protocols)
  • Other Conditions Requiring Daily Medication Administration
